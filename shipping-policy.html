Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Packaging

All orders are securely packed using high-quality packaging materials to ensure your products arrive fresh and intact. We take special care to protect food items during transit.

Dispatch

Orders are typically dispatched within 1–3 business days from the date of order confirmation and successful payment. Business days exclude Sundays and public holidays.

Delivery

  • Delivery time depends on the courier service and your destination location.
  • Estimated delivery is typically 3–7 business days after dispatch, depending on the pin code.
  • We ship pan-India.

Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, tracking details will be shared with you via your registered email or phone number. You can use the tracking ID to monitor the status of your shipment on the courier partner's website.

Delays

While we strive to ensure timely delivery, delays caused by courier service issues, natural events, weather conditions, or unforeseen circumstances are beyond our control. We appreciate your patience in such cases.
